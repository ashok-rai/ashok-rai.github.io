14-Day Trek Itinerary

Carefully planned route for optimal acclimatization and safety

Day 1: Arrival in Kathmandu

Airport pickup, hotel check-in, trek briefing and gear check

1,400m

Scenic mountain flight, begin trek through Sherpa villages

2,610m
Day 3-4: Trek to Namche Bazaar

Cross suspension bridges, enter Sagarmatha National Park

3,440m

Rest Day
